# Brand Guidelines Generator

Create comprehensive brand guidelines to ensure consistent visual and verbal identity.

## Overview

This skill helps you:

- Create brand style guides from scratch
- Document existing brand elements
- Define usage rules and examples
- Maintain brand consistency

## How to Use

### Create New Guidelines

```text
  "Create brand guidelines for [Company Name]"
  "Help me document our brand identity"
  "Generate a style guide based on our logo and colors"
```

### Update Existing

```text
  "Add social media guidelines to our brand guide"
  "Update our color palette documentation"
  "Define voice and tone guidelines"
```

## Brand Guidelines Structure

### Complete Guide Template

The template includes eight major sections:

1. **Brand Overview** - Mission, vision, values, personality, target audience
2. **Logo Usage** - Primary logo, variations, clear space, minimum sizes, color versions, do's/don'ts
3. **Color Palette** - Primary, secondary, and accent colors with hex/RGB/CMYK/Pantone values
4. **Typography** - Primary/secondary fonts, weights, sizes, line heights for desktop/mobile
5. **Imagery** - Photography style, illustration approach, iconography specifications
6. **Voice & Tone** - Brand voice attributes, tone by context, writing guidelines
7. **Applications** - Business cards, email signatures, social media specs, presentations
8. **Do's and Don'ts** - Best practices and common mistakes

## Quick Reference Card

Includes condensed brand information:

- Color palette summary with hex codes
- Font hierarchy
- Logo specifications (minimum size, clear space, formats)
- Core voice descriptors

## Limitations

- Cannot create visual assets like logos or images
- Color specifications require user verification
- Typography licensing remains the user's responsibility
- Legal review is recommended for usage rights
